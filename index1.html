Imran Khan

Imran Khan, full name Imran Ahmed Khan Niazi, is a Pakistani politician and former cricketer. He served as the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan from August 2018 until his removal in 2022, becoming the first prime minister to be ousted by a parliamentary vote. In addition to his political career, he was also a prominent cricketer, having captained the Pakistan national cricket team.
